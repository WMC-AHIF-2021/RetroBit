Quick Facts

  • Released: 1972
  • Developer: Atari
  • Studio: Atari
  • Genre: Sport/Retro
  • Consoles: Arcade, Meganfox Odyssey(first Console of Atari)

Gameplay

PONG's gameplay is actually fairly simple. It aims to replicate table tennis. The player controls a paddle (shown as elongated, vertical line) which can move up and down. The paddle on the opposite side is controlled by the second player. A player scores a point if the ball hits the wall at the opponent side. The goal is to reach 11 points and if that is achieved, that player wins.

Development

PONG was the first game developed by Atari. Allan Alcorn designed and developed PONG as an training exercise because he had no experience in video game development before that. Then PONG turned out to be so much fun, the founder of Atari (Nolan Bunshell) decided to launch the game.
