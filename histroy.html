    Pong

  • Realease: 1972
  • Developer: Atari
  • Studio: Atari
  • Genre: Sport/Retrogame
  • Consoles: Arcade, Meganfox Odyssey(first Console of Atari)

    • Pong is a two-dimensonal game, which simulates table tennis. Every player controls a rectangle on the left or right side of the screen, which can move verticaly. Players use the paddels to hit forth and back. If the ball reachs the end of the display, the side wherese ball comes from gets the point and the loser-side gets a freekick.
