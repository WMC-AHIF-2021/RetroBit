    Pong

  • Realease: 1972
  • Developer: Atari
  • Studio: Atari
  • Genre: Sport/Retrogame
  • Consoles: Arcade, Meganfox Odyssey(first Console of Atari)

    • Pong was the first game developed by Atari an d one of the oldest games, which are popular throughout the world.

    Gameplay

    Pong is a two-dimensonal game, which simulates table tennis. Every player controls a rectangle on the left or right side of the screen, which can move verticaly. Players use the paddels to hit forth and back. If the ball reachs the end of the display, the side wherese ball comes from gets the point and the loser-side gets a freekick.

Later Ping-Pong made so much fun, that Nolan Bunshell(fouder of Atari) decided to launch the game. As teh company Megenafox knew of the launch of Pong, they started a porcess aiganst Atari. Megnafox won it and Ataris had to pay $700.000. It was not a porblem for Atari, because they sold above 8000 Pong-arcadecoins.

    1974 Atari launched it Pong-console. At the the begin the console had few popularity, but it was completly succesfull at the christmas market. The Pong-console was succesfull until the beginn of the 1980 years.

    On the release of the Atari 2600 1977, Pong gets numerous variants.

    2012, on the occasion of 40th birthday of Pong, Atari released a new version of pong, which was developed by independece developer.

    Tetris

  • Realease: 1984
  • Developer: Alexy Pajtnov and Vladimir Pokhilko
  • Publisher(1996): Nintendo
  • Genre: Puzzle and title-matching
  • Console on frist-realease: IBM-PC

    • Gameplay

    Tetris has a field of play, where different geometric froms(tetrominoes) fall from the top of the field. The player can let the tetromino fall faster, rotate 90° right or left and let it fall to right, left or center.

    Histroy

    Tetris is a puzzle video game created in the USSR(Union of Socialist Soviet Republics). Alexy Pajtnoc wanted to recreated the game pentomies but he made a game of random pieces which the player will turn to fill the rows. Pajtnov named the game Tetris, a word from the combination tetra. The frist playable version has been launched on the June 6, 1984.

    1986 Tetris was published by the hungarian publisher Novotrade. The game was very succesfull and spread quickly to whole East-Europe. Without a sign of the Soviet Union, the rights of Tetris were sold to Microsoft and Spectrum Holobyte.

    Microsoft realesed their frist verion of Tetris on November 1987, the Spectrum Holobyte version was realesed it on 1988. Both verions of the game was for the IHM PC. Later Tetris was ported on platfroms including the AMiga, Atari ST, ZX Spectrum, Commodore 64 and Amstrad CD.

    In 1988 japanse rights were sold to Bullet Proof Software's. Inc and Atari Games.

    On June 1989 Nintendo and Atari Games began legal battle. The Judge decided in favor of Nintendo and therewith aiganst Atari. On the next day Atari withdrew its NES version from sale.

    Pajitnov and Henk Rogers founded The Tetris Company in June 1996 to manage on all rights on all platforms. After the dissoulution of the Soviet Union, it becomes a private company. In 2002 the Tetris Holding was founded and remvoes all unlicensed clones from the market.

    December 2005, Electronic Arts bought a company, which managed the Tetris license on mobile platforms. Following from this, Electronic Arts owns the rights of Tetris on the mobile market.
