Gameplay

The goal of Tetris is to get as many points as possible. This is achieved by stacking different shapes in the right way to complete a row. You get more points the more rows you remove at once. As the game goes on the speed of the blocks dropping in starts to rise. This results in a healthy curve of difficulty.

Development

Tetris was developed in a russian academy by Alexey Pajitnov. After many of his colleagues became addicted to this then new kind of game, he decided to release it by the hungarian publisher Novotrade. Later it was bought by Nintendo and released on various Nintendo Consoles. Today tetris is often used as a testing software and therefore ported to all kinds of platforms and devices.
